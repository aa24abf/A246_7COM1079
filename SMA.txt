1.SMAs are often used to determine trend direction. If the SMA is moving up, the trend is up. If the SMA is moving down, the trend is down. 

2.Shorter period SMAs can be used to determine shorter term trends.
3.SMAs are commonly used to smooth price data and technical indicators. The longer the period of the SMA, 
the smoother the result, but the more lag that is introduced between the SMA and the source.

4.Price crossing SMA is often used to trigger trading signals. When prices cross above the SMA, you might want to go long or cover short.
 when they cross below the SMA, you might want to go short or exit long.
